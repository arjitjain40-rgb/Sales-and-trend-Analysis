📊 E-commerce Sales & Profit Analysis
📌 Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to analyze sales and profitability trends across regions and product categories, and identify key factors affecting business performance.

📂 Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales data including:

Region
Category & Sub-category
Sales
Profit
Discount
Quantity
🔍 Analysis Performed
Sales by Region
Profit by Category
Discount vs Profit Analysis
Regional Profitability
Top Performing Products
📈 Key Insights
Profit declines significantly beyond a 20% discount threshold
Central region has highest sales but is loss-making
East and West regions are the most profitable
Furniture category generates high sales but low profit
Technology category is the most profitable
💡 Recommendations
Limit discounts to below 20%
Re-evaluate pricing in Furniture category
Reduce excessive discounting in Central region
Focus marketing efforts on Technology products
Expand strategies in profitable regions (East & West)
